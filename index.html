CATCHM: A novel network-based credit card fraud detection method using node representation learning

  • Network-based credit card fraud detection method using Representation Learning.
  • The relational structure of transactions can benefit fraud detection.
  • Our inductive extension helps meet the stringent time constraints in industry.
  • Artificial nodes in the network are beneficial for fraud detection.
  • Empirical evaluation on a real-life credit card dataset.
